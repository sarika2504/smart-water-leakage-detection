#Project Abstract

Water leakage is a major cause of water wastage in homes, industries, and agricultural systems. This project presents a Smart Water Leakage Detection System using Arduino UNO to monitor water flow continuously and detect leakage automatically.

The system uses two water flow sensors to compare the incoming and outgoing water flow. If a significant difference is detected, the Arduino identifies it as leakage and immediately switches OFF the water pump through a relay module. The current system status is displayed on a 16×2 LCD.

The project provides a simple, low-cost, and reliable solution for preventing unnecessary water loss. It can be further enhanced by integrating IoT technologies such as ESP32 or GSM modules to provide remote monitoring and instant alerts.

# Keywords

Arduino UNO, Water Flow Sensor, Relay Module, LCD Display, Water Leakage Detection, Embedded Systems, IoT.
